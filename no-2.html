American Lasagna

Lasagna is filling, relatively easy to assemble and make, highly adaptable and keeps well, making it an intriguing choice for restaurant chefs creating menus and home cooks alike

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ pounds lean ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 1 (29 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 12 dry lasagna noodles
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 pint part-skim ricotta cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  • In a skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef,after it starts to get some colour add in your onion and garlic and let it cook for about 5 minutes, drain out all excess fat from the meat. Add basil, oregano, brown sugar, 1 ½( teaspoons salt), diced tomatoes and tomato paste.Let it simmer for 30 to 45 minutes, stirring occasionally. While we wait for the sauce to simmer let's start making the lasagna noodles:bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil and add lasagna noodles, and cook for 5 to 8 minutes, or until dente; drain the water and rinse with cold water to prevent it from sticking together. Lay noodles flat on towels, and blot dry.
  • Next we make the ricotta filling for the lasagna:add the eggs into a bowl beat and add up the ricotta and parmesan cheese,parsley and salt,mix it all together and set it aside. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  • Now let’s assemble all of them together in our baking tray(dish).
    • First,start the base with a thin coating of sauce to avoid the noodles from sticking to the baking tray and layer 1/3 of the lasagna noodles in the bottom of the baking dish. Cover noodles with 1/2 ricotta mixture, 1/2 of the shredded mozzarella cheese and 1/3 of the sauce. Repeat the step until full. Top with remaining noodles and sauce. Sprinkle additional Parmesan cheese over the top.TIP Aluminum foil can be used to keep food moist, cook it evenly, and make clean-up easier Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. After which we will let it cool for 10 minutes.

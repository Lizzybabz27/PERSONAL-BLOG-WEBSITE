American Chop Suey

The name chop suey may look and sound chinese but its actually not, It's simply an American chop Suey. A very inexpensive easy to make dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5lbs - 80/20 Ground Beef
  • 2 tsp - Garlic (minced)
  • 1 medium - Onion (chopped)
  • 1 medium - Bell Pepper (chopped)
  • 3 TBS - Butter
  • 1 tsp - Basil
  • 1 tsp - Oregano
  • 1/2 tsp - Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp - Sugar
  • Kosher Salt to taste
  • 14.5 oz can - Tomato Sauce
  • 14.5 oz can - Diced Tomatoes or( you could chop them up yourself)
  • 6 oz - Tomato Juice
  • 1/4 Cup - Tomato Paste
  • 1 lb - Elbow Macaroni (cooked but not fully tender)

Directions

  • Ground beef in a large skillet on a medium high heat for about 5mins,when completely brown through, drain most of the fat off because you don't want too much fat in it so it doesn’t become greasy and not all the fat so it doesn;t become too dry.
  • Add butter, garlic, onions and bell pepper. sauté for about 2-3mins until the bell pepper and the onions begin to sweat.
    • Add remaining ingredients except pasta, bring to a boil and reduce the heat to medium low and simmer for about 25-30 minutes.
  • Add in the cooked elbow macaroni and mix well until it's all covered in the sauce, continue to simmer for about 5mins or until pasta is tender.
  • SERVE AND ENJOY YOUR AMERICAN CHOP SUEY ………

    • OVERALL COOK TIME...40MINS
